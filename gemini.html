The Clubs of VIT Pune

Discover a vibrant community of innovators, creators, and leaders. Find your passion beyond the classroom.

Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT) is renowned for its dynamic and diverse club culture. With over 40 technical, cultural, and social clubs, students have a platform to explore their passions. Technical clubs like the Robotics Forum, V-BUG (Linux User Group), and Team Veloce Racing participate in national competitions, fostering innovation. Cultural hubs like Antarang orchestrate major campus events. These student-run organizations are crucial for developing leadership, teamwork, and applying knowledge to real-world projects.
